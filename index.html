About Me

Hi, I'm Bijaya Shrestha—a Network Administrator and cybersecurity-focused IT professional originally from Sindhupalchok, Nepal, currently based in Canada. I specialize in managing and securing network infrastructure, with hands-on experience in routing, switching, troubleshooting, and system monitoring. I'm passionate about keeping systems reliable, secure, and running smoothly. I have a strong academic foundation in technology. Outside of work, I enjoy traveling and playing football, which help me stay active and connect with people from different cultures and backgrounds.
